Sneakers Boutique

Sneakers Boutique is a footwear shop wich includes the main functionalities of an online store application. The products are presented to the user by the home page, from wich he can see the details(price, description, materials) of the article that he is excited about. Of course, he can also introduce it in his cart and modify its amount afterwards. In addition to this, the admin page is the place where the footwear articles are added, modified or removed from the stocks.

This project was realised with Vanilla JavaScript and is connected to a Firebase database. It was used to store the products information wich was fetched from the frontend application by AJAX and APIs. I also uitilized Bootstrap and Media Queries to make the app responsive.
